Pizza

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Green bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Black olives
  • Italian sausage
  • Fresh basil
  • Garlic
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to the desired temperature (usually 450°F/230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza stone or a baking sheet.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your favorite pizza toppings, such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.
  7. Drizzle some olive oil over the toppings to enhance the flavor.
  8. Season the pizza with salt, pepper, and dried oregano or other preferred herbs.
  9. Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  10. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute or two.
  11. Slice the pizza and serve hot. Enjoy!
