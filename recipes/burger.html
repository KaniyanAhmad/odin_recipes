Burger

Ingredients

  • Beef patty
  • Burger bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Pickle
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bacon (optional)
  • Mushrooms (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients: beef patties, burger buns, lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, and any optional toppings you desire (bacon, mushrooms, etc.).
  2. Preheat a grill or a skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Season the beef patties with salt and pepper on both sides.
  4. Cook the beef patties on the grill or skillet for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your preferred level of doneness.
  5. If using cheese, add a slice of cheese on top of each patty during the last minute of cooking, and cover the patties to allow the cheese to melt.
  6. While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  7. Assemble the burger: Place a cooked patty on the bottom half of each bun.
  8. Add lettuce, tomato slices, onion rings, and pickles on top of the patty.
  9. Squeeze ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise (or any other sauces you prefer) over the toppings.
  10. If desired, add any optional toppings, such as bacon or saut√©ed mushrooms.
  11. Place the top half of the burger bun on the toppings to complete the burger.
  12. Serve the burger hot with your favorite side dishes. Enjoy!
